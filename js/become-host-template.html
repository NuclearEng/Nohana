Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to become a captain?

To become a captain, you need to own a boat, have valid registration and insurance, and pass our safety verification process. Your boat should be well-maintained and in good working condition.

How much can I earn?

Earnings vary based on your boat type, location, and availability. On average, hosts earn between $1,500 and $4,000 per month during peak season. You set your own prices and can adjust them anytime.

How does insurance work?

Nohana provides a $1M liability insurance policy that covers your boat during all bookings. This is included at no extra cost to you. We also verify all guests before they can book.

What fees does Nohana charge?

Nohana charges a 15% service fee on each booking. This covers payment processing, insurance, and platform maintenance. There are no upfront or monthly fees to list your boat.
